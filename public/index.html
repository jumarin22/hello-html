An Introduction

Two jobs ago I worked with a client who was born and raised in a different country. I don't remember which country they were from, but I'm pretty sure it was either Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, or Uzbekistan. They were going to visit home soon and wanted to be prepared for a familial tradition: playing chess against their brother. They asked me to play with them a few times before they left, and I agreed. We both downloaded the chess.com app and played a few games. Eventually I also purchased and provided a chess board for our office's front area where groups of clients frequently worked, socialized, and ate. The clients were all international, and it was interesting to see the game of chess providing a common thread between them.
